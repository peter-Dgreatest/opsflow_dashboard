# OpsFlow Knowledge Base

Welcome to **OpsFlow** — your all-in-one operations management platform. This guide will walk you through every feature and help you get the most out of the system.

---

## 🚀 Getting Started

### First Time Setup

1. **Sign Up** — Click "Create one" on the login page
2. **Enter Personal Details** — Full name, email, phone, password
3. **Verify Email** — Check your inbox for a 6-digit OTP code
4. **Set Up Business** — Add your business name, location, industry, and team size
5. **You're In!** — Start by adding customers and leads

### Logging In

- Enter your email and password on the login page
- Click "Forgot password?" if you need to reset your password
- An OTP code will be sent to reset your password

## 📋 Core Modules

### 1. Dashboard
The main overview page showing:
- **Total Revenue** — Your total income
- **Net Profit** — Revenue minus expenses
- **Outstanding** — Money still owed to you
- **Jobs This Month** — Active projects count
- **Financial Charts** — Revenue, profit, and expense trends
- **Jobs by Status** — Visual breakdown of all job statuses
- **Upcoming Jobs** — Next scheduled events
- **Recent Payments** — Latest payments received
- **Active Reminders** — Pending follow-ups

### 2. Jobs
Manage all your projects and events.

**Creating a Job:**
1. Click "New Job" button
2. Select a Customer (required)
3. Enter Job Title (required)
4. Set Start/End Dates
5. Choose Status (Planned, Confirmed, In Progress, Completed, Cancelled)
6. Add Quoted Price
7. Add Notes if needed

**Job Statuses:**
- **Planned** — Scheduled but not yet confirmed
- **Confirmed** — Client confirmed, ready to proceed
- **In Progress** — Currently being worked on
- **Completed** — Successfully finished
- **Cancelled** — No longer active

**Job Details:**
Click any job to see full details including:
- Customer information
- Payment history
- Expense tracking
- Revenue and profit calculations

### 3. Leads
Track prospective clients before they become customers.

**Lead Lifecycle:**
1. **Open** — New potential client
2. **Follow Up** — Needs additional contact
3. **Converted** — Became a paying customer
4. **Closed/Lost** — Deal didn't go through

**Converting a Lead:**
- Click "Convert" on any lead
- Link to existing customer or create new one
- Set up job details (title, dates, price)
- The lead becomes both a customer AND a job

### 4. Customers
Manage your client database.

**Adding a Customer:**
1. Click "Add Customer"
2. Enter Full Name (required)
3. Add Company Name (optional)
4. Add Phone and Email
5. Add any notes

**Customer Fields:**
- Full Name
- Company Name
- Phone Number
- Email Address
- Customer Code (auto-generated)
- Status (Active/Tentative)
- Created Date

### 5. Payments
Record all customer payments.

**Recording a Payment:**
1. Click "Record Payment"
2. Select the Job (customer auto-fills)
3. Enter Amount
4. Choose Payment Method
5. Set Payment Date
6. Add Notes (optional)

**Payment Methods:**
- Cash
- Bank Transfer
- POS
- Other

**Viewing Payment History:**
- See all payments in the table
- Filter by payment method
- View total received
- Track outstanding balances

### 6. Expenses
Track all business spending.

**Logging an Expense:**
1. Click "Log Expense"
2. Enter Purpose (e.g., "Crew transport")
3. Choose Category:
   - **Crew** — Staff payments
   - **Equipment** — Gear purchases/rentals
   - **Third Party** — Vendor services
   - **Misc** — Other expenses
4. Link to a Job (optional)
5. Enter Amount
6. Set Date
7. Add Notes

**Expense Categories:**
- Crew payments and wages
- Equipment purchases and rentals
- Third-party vendor costs
- Miscellaneous expenses

### 7. Crew
Manage your team members.

**Adding a Crew Member:**
1. Click "Add Crew"
2. Enter Name (required)
3. Add Role (e.g., "Mixing Engineer")
4. List Skills (comma-separated)
5. Add Phone and Email
6. Set Daily Rate
7. Add Notes

**Recording Crew Payments:**
1. Click "Record Payment"
2. Select Crew Member
3. Select the Job they worked on
4. Enter Amount
5. Choose Payment Method
6. Set Payment Date

### 8. Equipment
Track your inventory and assets.

**Adding Equipment:**
1. Click "Add Equipment"
2. Enter Name (e.g., "Sony EX1 Camera")
3. Add Category (e.g., "Video", "Audio", "Lighting")
4. Set Quantity
5. Choose Status (Active, Inactive, Deprecated)
6. Set Daily Rate
7. Enter Purchase Cost
8. Add Notes

**Equipment Status:**
- **Active** — Currently available
- **Inactive** — Temporarily unavailable
- **Deprecated** — No longer in use

### 9. Vendors
Manage third-party service providers.

**Adding a Vendor:**
1. Click "Add Vendor"
2. Enter Vendor Name (required)
3. Add Phone and Email
4. List Specialities (e.g., "Audio, LED Screens")
5. Add Summary/Description

**Common Vendor Types:**
- Audio equipment suppliers
- Lighting companies
- Transportation services
- Catering services
- Venue providers

### 10. Reminders
Never miss a follow-up or payment.

**Creating a Reminder:**
1. Click "Add Reminder"
2. Write the reminder message
3. Choose Type:
   - Client Payment
   - Crew Payment
   - Third Party Payment
   - Prospective Job
   - Other
4. Set Tag (Partial Payment, Overdue, Follow Up, etc.)
5. Link to a Job (optional)
6. Set Due Amount (optional)
7. Choose when to notify

**Managing Reminders:**
- View active reminders in card format
- Dismiss reminders when completed
- Delete reminders you no longer need
- Filter by status (Active, Dismissed, All)

## 🔑 Key Concepts

### Understanding Profit Margins

The system automatically calculates:
- **Revenue** — Total money received
- **Expenses** — Total spending
- **Profit** — Revenue minus expenses
- **Margin** — (Profit / Revenue) × 100%

**Margin Indicators:**
- 🟢 Green (60%+) — Excellent
- 🟡 Yellow (40-59%) — Good
- 🔴 Red (<40%) — Needs attention

### Job Workflow

```
Lead → Customer → Job → Payments → Expenses → Profit
         ↑
       Convert
```

1. Track potential clients as **Leads**
2. Convert to **Customers** when ready
3. Create **Jobs** for their projects
4. Record **Payments** received
5. Log **Expenses** incurred
6. View **Profit** automatically calculated

### Data Relationships

- **Jobs** belong to **Customers**
- **Payments** are linked to **Jobs**
- **Expenses** can be linked to **Jobs**
- **Crew** can be assigned to **Jobs**
- **Equipment** can be assigned to **Jobs**
- **Reminders** can reference **Jobs**

## 💡 Tips & Best Practices

### Getting Started Quickly

1. **Add your team first** — Crew members in Settings
2. **Import your equipment** — Know what you have
3. **Start with leads** — Build your pipeline
4. **Convert as you go** — Don't let leads stagnate

### Staying Organized

1. **Use reminders daily** — Check them every morning
2. **Log expenses immediately** — Don't wait until month-end
3. **Update job statuses** — Keep everyone informed
4. **Review reports weekly** — Catch issues early

### Maximizing Profit

1. **Track all expenses** — Nothing slips through
2. **Monitor margins** — Aim for 40%+
3. **Follow up on leads** — Convert quickly
4. **Review crew costs** — Optimize team usage

## 🔧 Troubleshooting

### Can't log in?
- Check your email/password
- Use "Forgot password" if needed
- Contact support if OTP doesn't arrive

### Not seeing data?
- Check your internet connection
- Refresh the page
- Clear browser cache

### Payments not updating?
- Ensure the job is selected correctly
- Check if amount is entered as a number
- Verify job exists and is active

### Report shows no data?
- Data populates as you use the system
- Check date range selected
- Ensure records have been created
